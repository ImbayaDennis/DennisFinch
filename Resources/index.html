Reflection

My experience as an intern at Elewana's Loisaba Tented Camp has been one riddled with unexpected but pleasant twists in my expectations. I have learned a lot of invaluable lessons that have pushed me to become better in various skills including but not limited to communication and observation. The one lesson that I personally believe has had the most impact in my life thus far is to put myself out there and not be afraid to make mistakes as that is the fastest way to learn. Sure, this is something you are encouraged to do everyday, but this has been particularly difficult for me as I am somewhat of a perfectionist and I have to make sure that whatever I'm doing is bound to succeed. But with the overwhelming support I received from the staff, which frankly I did not expect at first, I was able to push past this block and the results were more than pleasant.
